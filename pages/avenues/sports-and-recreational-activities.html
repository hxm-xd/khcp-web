Sports and Recreational Activities

Teamwork & Well-being

The Sports and Recreational Activities avenue focuses on promoting physical well-being, teamwork, and camaraderie among members through various sports events and recreational activities. We believe that a healthy body fosters a healthy mind and stronger bonds.

What We Do

We organize sports tournaments, friendly matches, hiking trips, and other recreational outings that allow members to unwind, stay active, and build lasting friendships outside of formal meetings.
